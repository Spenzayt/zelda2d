## Overview

**The Legend of Zelda Like** is a 2D adventure game inspired by *The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past*. You play as **Link**, exploring Hyrule, fighting enemies, and collecting items to ultimately defeat **Ganon** and save Princess Zelda.  

- Created in 1 week by [Romain](https://github.com/Spenzayt), [Léa](https://github.com/LeaPav) and [Tristan](https://github.com/Tristan94-Germain).

## Features

- **Exploration**: Travel through a part of the map of Zelda - A Link to the Past :  
  - **Link’s House**: Starting point of the adventure.  
  - **Forest and Clearing**: Solve puzzles and find the legendary sword.  
  - **Hyrule Castle**: Confront Ganon and complete your quest.  
- **Enemies**: Face multiple types of foes:  
  - **Patrolling**: Move along a set path.  
  - **Chaser**: Hunt the player when nearby.  
  - **Archer**: Attack from a distance.  
- **Combat & Interaction**:  
  - Swing your sword to defeat enemies.  
  - Pull bushes to uncover secrets.  
- **Inventory System**: Collect items and manage them with a dedicated interface.  
- **Customizable Controls**: Default keys are ZQSD to move, I for inventory, right-click to attack, left-click to interact with objects, but they can be changed.

---

## Gameplay

1. **Start the Game**: Begin in Link’s house.  
2. **Explore Hyrule**: Move through forests, clearings, and the castle, encountering enemies and obstacles.  
3. **Fight Enemies**: Use your sword and strategy to defeat patrollers, chasers, and archers.  
4. **Collect Items**: Find the sword and other objects to enhance your abilities.  
5. **Save Hyrule**: Defeat Ganon and rescue Princess Zelda to win the game.
